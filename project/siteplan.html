Website Site Plan: Electromovil

1. Site Name

Name: Electromovil

Reason for Selection: The name combines "electro" (reference to electric technology) and "movil" (mobility), directly representing electric cars. It is concise, memorable, and clearly describes the website's focus.

Potential Domain Availability: electromovil.org

2. Site Purpose

The website offers an educational and informative platform about electric cars, providing:

  • Detailed information about electric vehicle technology
  • Analysis of environmental benefits
  • Guide for potential buyers
  • News and industry trends
  • Interactive savings calculator

3. User Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: How much could I save by switching from a gasoline car to an electric one?

    Answer: Savings calculator tool with comparison of fuel costs, maintenance, and environmental impact.

  2. Scenario 2: What electric car models currently exist in the market?

    Answer: Catalog section with information about different brands, ranges, prices, and features.

4. Color Scheme

Green (#2ecc71)

Primary Color - Represents sustainability

Usage: Headings, main buttons

Blue (#3498db)

Secondary Color - Suggests technology and innovation

Usage: Section backgrounds, links

5. Typography

  • Headings: Roboto Bold

    Conveys modernity and clarity

  • Body Text: Open Sans

    Readability and neutrality for extensive content

  • Accent Elements: Montserrat

    For calls to action and highlighted elements

6. Wireframe Structure

Note: A detailed diagram will be created in a design tool. This is a textual description:

Mobile View:

  • Hamburger menu at the top
  • Main title with background image
  • Vertically stacked sections:
    • Introduction to electric cars
    • Savings calculator
    • Model catalog
    • News blog
  • Footer with links and social media

Desktop View:

  • Horizontal navigation menu
  • Main title with full-screen background image
  • Column layout:
    • Left column: Introduction
    • Central column: Calculator
    • Right column: Blog highlights
  • Sections with grid design
  • Expanded footer with more information
